Tragedy as lovers got stuck together while having sex and died later, inside a car

Lovers as the persons could not be identified and there was no ring on the both of their fingers, suggested to passerbys who were there at the scene of the unfortunate incident that they weren't married. The lovers died on the 20th of January 2022 while having sex( populary called "muegbesuebe" by the Benin Nigerians). It was later discovered that the woman was the wife of a chief. This said chief, had placed " voodoo"( curse that of the wife should sleep with anyone, she and the said man would die. The body of the housewife wasn't claimed by the husband as this was an abomination to him. Her body was later claimed by her parents to bury.
